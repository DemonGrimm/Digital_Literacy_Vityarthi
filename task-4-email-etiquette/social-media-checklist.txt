Responsible Social Media Use Checklist (For College Students)

✅ Do’s
Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts and comments
Think before posting—ensure your content reflects your values and goals
Use strong privacy settings to protect personal information
Share accurate and verified information only
Build a positive digital presence by sharing achievements, projects, and skills

❌ Don’ts
Don’t share sensitive personal information (address, phone number, passwords)
Avoid posting offensive, inappropriate, or harmful content
Don’t engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
Avoid accepting requests from unknown or suspicious accounts
Don’t spend excessive time on social media at the cost of studies
