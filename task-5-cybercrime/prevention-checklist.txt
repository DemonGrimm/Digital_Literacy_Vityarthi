Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)
✅ General Safety Tips
1. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts; avoid using the same password everywhere
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts like email, banking, and social media
3. Avoid clicking on unknown links or attachments received via email, SMS, or WhatsApp
4. Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software
5. Limit the personal information you share on social media (location, phone number, daily routine)
6. Always use secure Wi-Fi networks; avoid logging into sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi
7. Verify the authenticity of websites before entering details (check for https:// and correct domain names)

💳 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
8. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank
9. Always double-check payment requests—remember, receiving money does NOT require entering a PIN
10. Avoid installing apps or APK files from unknown sources that ask for banking permissions

🚨 If You Face Cybercrime
Report immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for urgent financial fraud assistance
