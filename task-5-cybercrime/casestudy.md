#CASE STUDY: - UPI / Online Payment Fraud#
UPI fraud is a problem in India. People are getting tricked into paying money or 
sharing their banking information. There was a case in The Times of India where 
scam people made fake websites and messages about LPG bookings.
They sent messages to people saying things like "you have not paid for your 
LPG" or "you need to update your information". Here is how the scam worked: 
first the scam people sent a message with a link, to a website. Then they asked 
people to pay money using UPI or to download an app. When people did this the 
scam people got into their bank accounts. Got their secret codes. Sometimes the 
scam people could even control the person’s phone. Take their money.
These scams usually happen to people like students and people who work. They 
often target people who do not know much about keeping themselves online. 
When this happens people can lose money have their identity stolen and someone 
else can get into their bank accounts or messaging apps.
This case shows us that being careless online can be very bad. It could have been 
stopped if people checked the websites did not click on unknown links and did 
not approve payments that they were not expecting. We need to be aware and 
careful when we're online to stop these kinds of UPI fraud. UPI fraud is an issue 
and we should all be careful to avoid UPI fraud
